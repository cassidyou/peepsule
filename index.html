What is Peepsule all about?

Peepsule is a decentralized social network platform that rewards people for using the platform, protecting their privacy and rewarding them for engaging. Psule token Is the native token built for the peepsule network on the Cardano blockchain that connects people to a decentralized social media platform that reward users for using.

Why do peepsule have value?

The value of Peepsule is determined by the users and the user case of the Peepsule Network, we are solving a social network problem by giving people value for their time, in the nearest future we are integrating gaming and NFT to the platform to create strategic solutions for our ecosystem.

What are the benefits of holding PSule token?

The PSule token being the native currency of the Peepsule network can be used by user for Content tokenization allowing users to send awards and tokens to one another to show appreciation for posts, can be used to access the NFT, Gaming and marketplace portal of the platform. When listed on an exchange, it can be exchanged with other cryptocurrencies.
